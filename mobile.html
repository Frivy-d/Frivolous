ARTISTSTATEMENT

My inspiration for this project is born from a desire to weave two divergent forces together: craft with the nerve of exploration. Magic and impulse converge into a unified field by exploring the intersections of chaotic coincidence and subtle historical traditions. Before I proceed, two common objections should be raised: that not every intuition can translate to crafted work — and yet certain works demand exactly that tension.
